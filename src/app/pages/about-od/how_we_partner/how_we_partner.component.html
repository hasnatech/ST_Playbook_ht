The Talent Performance (TP) domain is responsible for overseeing the design and delivery of business specific TP solutions, including consultative services to support the needs of the business and to help Organization create a talent-based competitive advantage. This includes ensuring alignment of activities with the global TP strategy and programs.

This domain facilitates and delivers corporate TP solutions and processes areas as defined by the Talent COE.

The focus of Organization Development (OD) COE is providing thought-leadership as well ownership of strategy and solution design around the OD specific practice areas. We work closely with the Talent COE partners, but also with partners beyond the TP COE domain, not limiting to:

  • Senior Leadership Group
  • Enterprise & Function BHR
  • Transformation Management Office and Transformation Office (for example People Leads)
  • GHRS TP Domain (see below)

The GHRS TP Domain oversees the delivery of business specific talent performance solutions, including consultative services to support the needs of the business. This includes ensuring alignment of activities with the global talent performance strategy and programs, along with focus on continuous improvement and inclusion and diversity. Some key areas:

  • Organization Development (OD) (e.g. Culture, Leadership/Team Engagement, Culture, Change Management, Organization Design)
  • Talent Management (e.g. Talent Discussions, Talent Development, Recognition, Inclusion & Diversity strategy, etc)
  • Learning & Development (e.g. Education & Learning strategy, Capability Building, Leadership/Team Development, Coaching/Mentoring, etc)

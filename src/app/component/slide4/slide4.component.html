Our Main Purpose

This playbook has been created to guide you through the relevant content regarding Strategic People Transformations with a key focus on People. Our main goal is to provide you guidance and insights in the world of Organization Development (OD) and transformation, existing processes and relevant tools and links which help you drive people transformations in your own area.

This Playbook will support in…

  • … Providing more context on OD and how we partner with our transformation related colleagues
  • … Clarifying the various OD practice areas and how we work together, mainly focusing on how Strategic People Transformation ties together the various areas
  • …Elaborating on the Transformation Management Office and how Strategic People Transformation works together with the TMO People Leads
  • ... Providing relevant tools which are applicable in assessing/scoping, monitoring and reviewing people related transformations

For whom is this Playbook intended?

The playbook addresses OD practitioners and non-OD practitioners, who are (in)directly involved in driving Strategic People Transformation activities and focuses on the People aspect of the transformation.

The key user groups we have identified for this Playbook, but not limiting to, are:

  • OD Core team (COE & GHRS)
  • TMO People Leads
  • OD Key Partners (e.g. L&D, Corporate Affairs, BHR, TP Consultants, BRM, TP COE partners, etc)
  • Transformation Leaders across the organization

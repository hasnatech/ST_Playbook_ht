The TO’s are key in delivering the transformation success and among the various roles the People Leads play an eminent role, guided by the OD COE, in determining the Strategic People Transformation framework across the organization and to foster alignment, facilitate knowledge sharing and build capability.

People Lead

  1. Develop Ent/Fun and Group/Sub-Function level Stakeholder Management plans based on transformation roadmaps
    • Work with TLs to develop and plan the right engagements to support transformation objectives
    • Pulse leaders and key stakeholders to identify risk areas and readiness to transform
  2. Build awareness across organization to change the way we work
    • ACI, PPM and other value levers (e.g. CBS) that are new to the organization
  3. Develop, refine or validate (depending upon where they are in their transformation efforts) the overall “change agenda story” in partnership with communications.
    • Coach senior leads to effectively tell the story and lead through change
  4. Assess HR needs in support of TMO roadmap and partner with GHRS to diagnose (also see the Strategic People Transformation Process)
    • Submit demand through HR intake process to prioritize and fulfill resource requests
    • Align HR projects and resources to drive coordinated approach to change
  5. Participate in TO cadence of Enterprise or Function:
    • Identify new HR needs and support
    • Articulate health of programs/projects with HR workstreams
    • Escalating people related issues or risks to overall transformation effort
    • Identify possible collisions in roadmap impacting people and our ability to change

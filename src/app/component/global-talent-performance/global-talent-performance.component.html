GHRS Talent Performance

The GHRS Talent Performance Domain oversees the delivery of business-specific talent performance solutions, including consultative services to support the needs of the business.

This includes ensuring alignment of activities with the global talent performance strategy and programs, along with focus on continuous improvement and inclusion and diversity.

The Talent Performance Domain consists of three practice areas:

  • Talent Management delivers integrated talent solutions to drive talent strategies by leading succession planning, talent assessment and development to support the management and growth of talent pools
  • Organization Development supports the development of organization structures, identifying cultural impacts, leading change efforts and building high performing leadership teams to drive business results
  • Learning & Development facilitates L&D strategy through corporate, regional and need-based learning programs to develop core business, leadership and functional capabilities of our managers and employees.

Does your business need support for Talent Performance (TP) services? Not sure how and when to partner with TP? Here is an new fact sheets for more in-depth details on each service.

To engage with TP on new project requests, follow the process below:

  • BHR work with your BRM (Business Relationship Manager) to initiate and prioritize all project requests including change management
  • BRM will work with GHRS to review project requests to determine capacity and budget availability

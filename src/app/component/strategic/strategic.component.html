Strategic People Transformation is one of the OD Practice Areas and covers the diagnosis of organizational shifts which have a people impact and is needed to achieve transformational objectives and advises on pacing, risk mitigation and actions that result in sustainable change.

How is strategy defined in Organization?

A strategy can be defined as a long term plan to help achieve an organization’ objectives. It states how business should be conducted to achieve desired goals and is managed through a strategic framework.

Cargill’s Game Plan for Success 2.0 is a great real example of a strategic framework to align the whole company on where we are heading and how we will get there.

How does Strategic People Transformation support?

When defining the companywide as well as business specific priorities, it is key to start transforming the organizational capabilities to translate our business strategy into execution.

It is our purpose to assess high impact/priority transformations with impact on people and help bring together our OD practices in a holistic people transformation approach, ensuring a fit for purpose and value driven approach that aligns to the overall HR strategy

 

When is Strategic People Transformation critical?

Strategic People Transformation is critical in the following situations:

  • When collaborating with People Leads to understand and anticipate on the OD needs following portfolio rationalization activities
  • In transformations with high workforce impacts and supporting in transformation programs supporting new (cross) Business Strategies, Leadership Teams, and/or Operating Model transformations
  • When creating an overall OD transformation approach cross businesses and partnering with the various OD practices ensuring an integrated way of working.

Strategic People Transformation OD Practice Area

Focusing on the people aspect of transformation, this specific practice area is key in integrating all other OD practice areas when looking at high impact, critical transformations across our organization.

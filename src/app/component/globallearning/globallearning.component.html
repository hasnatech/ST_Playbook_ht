Global Learning & Development (L&D)

Learning & Development designs and delivers modern, personalized learning experiences that enable individuals and teams to learn or deepen the capabilities needed for Organization to achieve its growth aspirations, compete, remain relevant and solve challenges today and in the future.

With the pace and volume of change rapidly increasing, and the degree of uncertainty we face in today’s global business environment, we must recognize the critical role organizational learning can and must play in Organization’s success.

New learner-centric design methods and digital learning technologies allow us to deliver high-impact learning experiences that:

  • more closely mimic how we learn in our social lives;
  • integrate with employees’ daily flow of work simply in an accessible way;
  • require shorter design cycles;
  • are more scalable but highly personalized;
  • largely eliminate the need for travel;
  • reduce the global footprint to support and execute (e.g., L&D, coordination, and IT resources); and
  • enable learning anytime, anywhere, and is accessible through any device.

Your learning Anytime Your Way

Our digital learning vision and strategy is driving the modernization of the employee learning experience at Organization and positioning corporate learning investments as a lever to accelerate transformation and execute on strategies.

Organization Learning & Development (L&D) Governance: Creating a culture of continuous learning across Organization

Organization’s Learning & Development (L&D) Governance serves as organizational guardrails for how Organization learning assets are managed. By aligning to these standards, all areas across Organization that manage some form of learning, training, or education will be doing their part to optimize the employee learning experience, maximize the return on Organization’s investments, and mitigate risks associated with IP and data privacy.

There are three Organization L&D Governance Operating Parameters that make up the governance. As defined in these operating parameters, best practice is to leverage existing learning experiences and technologies whenever possible. This ensures greater efficiency and effectiveness for all of Organization. More information can be found on each of the operating parameters here:

  • Curriculum Ownership & Learning Content Management
  • Learning Technology & Design Standards
  • Learning Contracts & Select Suppliers

My Learning
at cargil

My Learning, powered by Degreed as the underlying technology, is the single point of entry for all types of learning opportunities across Organization, such as Degreed plans and pathways, collaborative learning (e.g., Manager Essentials, Unconscious Bias, Sales Excellence, Intro to Supply Chain), and Compliance, Safety and Regulatory training.

My Learning simplifies the employee experience by providing one click registration for collaborative learning opportunities.

Consolidating to one single source will enable more robust data and analytics on learner behavior, consumption and completions regardless of where the learning is occurring or what technology is being used.

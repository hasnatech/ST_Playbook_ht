Elevating our capabilities to enable transformation at Organization will be one of the core focus points moving forward. Partnering with Global Learning & Development and the transformation initiatives SMEs, the Transformation Capability Platform is the one-stop shop to help grow our people in leading, delivering and executing transformation.

The capabilities we need to enable transformation are aligned to our strategy and are grouped into three categories:

  • Transformational Leadership includes transformation/change leadership and financial acumen.
  • Transformation Enablers includes digital, data, integrated business planning (IBP), commercial excellence (CE), supply chain design and optimization (SCDO), sourcing and procurement (CSSP), R&D/Innovation, Organization business services (CBS), organization design and manufacturing excellence (ME).
  • Transformation Execution includes accelerated continuous improvement (ACI), portfolio & project management (PPM), value creation and agile.

This site is here to help you learn more about our teams, the way we work and the value we will deliver.
